Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

We carefully vet all our suppliers and products. Each item comes with detailed information about its environmental impact, materials used, and certifications. We prioritize products with third-party certifications like GOTS, Fair Trade, and FSC.

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in their original packaging. Returns are free for defective items. For other returns, customers pay shipping costs. All returns must be approved before shipping.

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. We use eco-friendly packaging and carbon-neutral shipping partners whenever possible.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history.

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After that, please contact us immediately. Once an order ships, it cannot be cancelled but can be returned per our return policy.
