Premium Organic Cotton T-Shirt

Our organic cotton t-shirt is crafted with the highest standards of sustainability and comfort. Each shirt is made from 100% GOTS certified organic cotton — no harmful chemicals, no pesticides, just pure natural fibre.

The fabric is naturally soft, breathable, and durable. Classic fit, reinforced stitching, comfortable neckline. Available in a range of earth-friendly colors that are dyed with AZO-free, OEKO-TEX certified dyes.
