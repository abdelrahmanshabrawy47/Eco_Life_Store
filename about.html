About Eco Life Store

Creating a sustainable future, one eco-friendly product at a time

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Eco Life Store was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to make sustainable living accessible and affordable for everyone. What started as a small online shop in Cairo has grown into a movement dedicated to reducing our environmental footprint.

We believe that small changes in our daily choices can create a big impact on our planet. That's why we carefully curate products that are not only good for you but also good for the earth — from organic cotton clothing to zero-waste personal care items.

Our journey began when our founder, AbdElrahman Shabrawy, realised the devastating impact of fast fashion and disposable products on our environment and decided to take action by connecting conscious consumers with ethical brands and sustainable alternatives.

Our Values

These principles guide everything we do

Sustainability First

Every product we offer meets strict environmental standards. We prioritise materials and processes that minimise harm to our planet.

Ethical Sourcing

We work directly with fair-trade certified suppliers and support communities that share our commitment to ethical business practices.

Quality & Transparency

We believe in complete transparency about our products' origins, materials, and environmental impact. Quality is never compromised.

Community Impact

For every purchase, we contribute to environmental conservation projects and support local communities in sustainable development.
